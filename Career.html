Career

Growing as a boy here in Lesotho is basically steriotypical, in fact, for
every Mosotho child. Establishing a different career choice other than through
school is almost mystrical. My career path is no different from every other
child's growing up in Lesotho.

I have gone to elementry, middle school, high school and to where i am
now-varsity.The road has been pretty tough getting here but like any
purposeful person i have rode my way here and i take pride in every step
it took to get here
